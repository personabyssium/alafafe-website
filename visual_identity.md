# Visual Identity: Alafafe Cabinet de Courtage

**Concept:** Professional & Sober Integrity
**Keywords:** Reliability, Protection, Authority, Stability.

---

## 1. Refined "Sober" Color Palette

| Color | Hex Code | Usage | Mood |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Deep Forest Emerald** | `#004D3F` | Primary Brand Color (Logo & Navigation) | Institutional, Solid, Trustworthy |
| **Slate Navy** | `#1E293B` | Main Text, Heavy Accents | Professional, Quiet Authority |
| **Silk Silver** | `#E2E8F0` | Subtle Accents, Dividers, Secondary UI | Clean, Precise, Modern |
| **Pure White** | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, Negative Space | Transparency, Clarity |

> [!NOTE]
> We have opted for a more restrained use of color, leaning into the Deep Emerald and Slate Navy for a corporate, "sober" feel.

---

## 2. Typography (Clean & Geometric)

### Heading Font: **Outfit**
- **Weight:** Semi-Bold (for hierarchy), Regular (for clarity).
- **Usage:** "Alafafe" Logotype and Page Headings.

### Body Font: **Inter**
- **Weight:** Regular, Medium.
- **Usage:** All operational content, forms, and technical descriptions.

---

## 3. Selected Logo: The "Negative Space Shield"

**Visual Description:**
- A solid, minimalist shield icon in **Deep Forest Emerald**.
- Within the shield, the letter **"A"** is carved out using negative space (Pure White).
- The shield represents the insurance "protection" layer.
- Adjacent to or below the shield, the word **ALAFEFE** is set in **Outfit** (Semi-Bold) with generous letter spacing to convey elegance and room to breathe.

**Symbolism:**
- **The Shield:** Universal symbol of protection and peace of mind.
- **Negative Space A:** Suggests that the agency (A) is at the heart of the protection provided.

---

## 4. Design Principles
- **Minimalism:** Minimal use of shadows or gradients; flat or very subtle "material" depth.
- **Alignment:** Strict grid-based layout to reflect organizational discipline.
- **Visual Weight:** Heavy use of whitespace to ensure information is easy to digest without feeling cluttered.
