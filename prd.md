# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Alafafe Assurance

**Project Name:** Alafafe Cabinet de Courtage d'Assurance Website
**Target Market:** Morocco (Individuals and Businesses)
**Objective:** Establish a modern, trustworthy, and conversion-oriented digital presence for a new insurance brokerage agency in Morocco.

---

## 1. Competitive Analysis & Inspiration
Based on research of market leaders like **AFMA** and **Ask Assurance**, the website should prioritize:
- **Trust & Credibility:** Professional imagery, clear partner logos (AXA, Sanlam, etc.), and legal disclosures.
- **Bilingual Accessibility:** (Differentiator) Offering the site in both **French** and **Arabic** to cater to the entire Moroccan demographic.
- **Conversion Tools:** WhatsApp integration (primary in Morocco) and easy-to-use quote request forms.

---

## 2. Core Features (Functional Requirements)

### A. Information Architecture
- **Home:** Hero section with clear UVP (Unique Value Proposition), partner carousel, and quick links.
- **Particuliers (Individuals):** Sub-pages for Auto, Multi-Risque Habitation (MRH), Health, and Travel insurance.
- **Professionnels (Business):** Sub-pages for RC Professionnelle, Fleet insurance, and Employee health schemes.
- **Sinistres (Claims):** A "What to do?" guide for accidents, including emergency numbers.
- **Contact:** Interactive map (Casablanca/Local), contact form, and direct dial buttons.

### B. Interactive Features
- **WhatsApp Bridge:** A floating action button for instant messaging with a broker.
- **Quote Request Engine:** Multi-step forms tailored to the type of insurance (e.g., car brand/year for Auto).
- **Partner Showcase:** Scrolling banner featuring major Moroccan insurance companies.

---

## 3. Design & UX (Non-Functional Requirements)
- **Aesthetic:** "Glassmorphism" or clean "Neo-Corporate" style. Use deep blues, emerald greens (common in Moroccan trust symbols), and high-contrast whites.
- **Performance:** Fast loading on mobile (4G/5G Moroccan networks).
- **SEO:** Optimized for keywords like "Courtier assurance Casablanca", "Assurance auto Maroc", etc.

---

---

## 5. Technology Stack (Recommendation)
- **Frontend:** Next.js or Vite (React/Tailwind) for speed and SEO.
- **CMS:** Headless CMS (like Sanity or Strapi) or simple markdown-based content for easy updates.
- **Deployment:** Vercel or local Moroccan hosting (if data sovereignty is a concern).
